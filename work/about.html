About Eriklassic

Abuja‑based fashion brand where African elegance meets affordability.

Abuja • Nigeria

Eriklassic World of Fashion is a Nigerian fashion house dedicated to celebrating African elegance with a modern, affordable twist.

From rich Adire fabrics and aso oke to bespoke ready‑to‑wear pieces, shoes and handbags, every item is curated to help you stand out without overspending.

Whether you are dressing for work, church, owambe, weddings or everyday slay, Eriklassic brings you looks that feel comfortable, confident and uniquely you.
